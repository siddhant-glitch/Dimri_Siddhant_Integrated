Seasons

29/9/2019


Summer is when you Spring into life. Winter is when you Fall. Summer is when you evoke the spirit of renewal in me. Winter is when you commence the ritual of abandoning me. Summer is when you Bloom and I start Flowering. Winter is when you Wilt and I start Withering. Summer is when you are warm and relaxing but I get sunburnt effortlessly. Winter is when you are cold and comforting but I get sweeped by snowstorms facilely. Summer is when you are consumed by happiness. Winter is when you devour me of mine. Summer is when you face the mirror and reflect the yellow and the bright. Winter is when you face the wall and paint it blue and lifeless. Summer. Winter. Winter. Summer. 6 months. 6 months. 6 weeks. 6 weeks. 6 hours. 6 hours. 6 minutes. 6 minutes. You. Me. Me. You.
