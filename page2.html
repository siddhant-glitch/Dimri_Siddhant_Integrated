An Ode to Clouds

29/9/2019


Time stops when I see you My heart stops when I see you There's peace and silence. Time stops when I see you. I can look at you for hours and the million forms you take, I can find beauty in each. My sight expands as you take up a new shape every second. Blue and white feel more colourful to me than any rainbow I have seen. Try stopping for once, try stopping your heart for once. You'll realise we both are one.
