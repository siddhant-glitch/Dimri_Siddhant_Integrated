Time

03/09/2019


Don't wait. Time stops for none. You are at a beach and you want to hold this memory forever. You take a handful of sand in your hand and close your fist. You make efforts to keep it in your hand, in your heart, in your memory, and it keeps running down your fingers. It continues till there's nothing left of what you begin with.
